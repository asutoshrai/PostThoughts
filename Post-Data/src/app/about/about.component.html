About Us

Kind Acts is a project to engage students into the everyday acts of kindness. Encouraging kindness in children has already proven to result in higher cooperation, concentration and confidence in children. If we want our children to live a happy life with healthy relationships, our schools and homes must provide them opportunity to experience kindness and giving. KEY is one of our initiative help our children to experience and learn Kindness, Empathy and Love.
Few of Our Activities in schools: 1. Wall Of Kindness 2. 21 Days Kindness Challenge 3. The Kindness Wallet…(you are here) 4. Embracing Happiness by Forgiveness
KindActs.in is run purely by volunteers. We would love to grow kindness tribe on mother earth. If you like to be part of any of these initiative, feel free to connect with us.
